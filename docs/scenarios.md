 Scenario 2 – Event Organizer (Raj)
Raj owns a cozy café in the city and hosts open mic nights every weekend. He wants to attract more local performers and audiences. One afternoon, he logs into the Local Event Finder platform to create a new event. The interface guides him through entering event details — title, date, time, and a short description. He uploads a photo of his café and selects “Music & Performance” as the category. The system automatically suggests hashtags and nearby influencers who might help promote the event. Raj appreciates the smart suggestions but feels overwhelmed by the analytics dashboard showing engagement metrics and ticket trends. He decides to focus on the basics — posting the event and sharing it on social media through the integrated share button. Within hours, he receives notifications that several users have bookmarked his event.
 Scenario Elements
Context: Raj, café owner, creating and promoting an event.
Activity: Using the event creation and promotion tools.
Problem/Gap: Analytics dashboard feels too complex.
Outcome: Successfully posts and promotes the event; gains visibility among local users
